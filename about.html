About Me

My name is Anna; I’m 26 years old and based in Stockholm. I completed a bachelor’s degree in IT, Media, and Design at Södertörn University, a broad program where I gained experience in UX design, interaction design, web design, and media production. I became particularly interested in UX design, which led me to pursue a master’s degree in User Experience and Interactive Media Design, also at Södertörn University.

I also discovered a passion for programming, so I decided to code my own portfolio from scratch. This is the result!
