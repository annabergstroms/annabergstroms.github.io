Wise

Take control and manage your money



Project Overview

Summary and Challenge

In recent years, more and more young adults have struggled with managing their finances, particularly saving, despite the availability of finance apps. Most young adults don’t have large savings or the ability to save large sums; instead, they save smaller amounts regularly.

For my master's thesis, I developed Wise, a personal finance management app designed to meet the needs and preferences of young adults. I conducted a competitive analysis and semi-structured interviews with the target group. Using the interview data, I created a prototype, which was then tested and refined based on user feedback.
